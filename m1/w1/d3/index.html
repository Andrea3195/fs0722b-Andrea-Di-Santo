Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

This recipe is for an incredibly and tasty focaccia with caramelized onions, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter

FEAUTURED IN:
Fresh Cranberries Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixed bowl, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully abosrbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, tought I sometimes knead it a few time in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
